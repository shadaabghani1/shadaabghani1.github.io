I am an AI Engineer and Licentiate Researcher at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. My work focuses on trustworthy LLM systems, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), hallucination detection, source attribution, and agentic AI.

I work on the design of grounded and reliable AI systems for real-world use, combining research with hands-on engineering across backend services, retrieval pipelines, orchestration, and deployment.

I am currently employed at KTH in Department of Computing and Learning Systems. Alongside my research, I build production-oriented AI systems, including SPARA, an AI system for building energy-efficiency advising for housing cooperatives in Stockholm.

## Research Interests

- Trustworthy LLM systems
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Hallucination detection and source attribution
- Agentic AI and orchestration workflows
- Runtime monitoring and evaluation of LLM systems
- Applied AI for sustainability and decision support
